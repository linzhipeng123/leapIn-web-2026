# <center>LeapIn Website Privacy Policy</center>

<p align="right">Last updated April 11, 2026</p>

Important Notice

This Privacy Policy applies to personal data collected when you visit the LeapIn official website, including use of our AI assistant Elsa, submission of your resume, and requests for a product demonstration.

If you are a LeapIn enterprise customer or a candidate participating in a recruitment process through the LeapIn platform, your data relationship with the product is separately governed by the LeapIn Product User Agreement and LeapIn Product Privacy Policy.

### 1. What We Collect and Why

We collect personal data in the following three scenarios when you visit this website.

#### 1.1 Resume Submission

When you submit a resume through our website, we collect the information you provide, including your name, contact details, educational background, and work experience. We use this information to process your application and contact you where necessary.

#### 1.2 Demo Request

When you complete a demo request form, we collect your name, company name, and email address. We use this information to arrange the demonstration and follow up on your enquiry.

#### 1.3 Using AI Assistant Elsa

When you interact with Elsa, your conversation content is processed to generate responses. Elsa is powered by Alibaba Cloud's Qwen-Max model. Your conversation data is processed by Alibaba Cloud through its international deployment, with endpoints and data storage located in Singapore. Alibaba Cloud's privacy policy is available on its official website.

We use conversation data solely to provide immediate responses. We do not use it for model training or sell it to third parties. Elsa's responses are for reference only and do not constitute legal, commercial, recruitment, or other professional advice.

### 2. Who Processes Your Data and Where It Is Stored

Resume and demo request data you submit is stored in our own CRM system, with servers located in Singapore.

We do not sell, rent, or disclose your personal data to any other third party, except in the following circumstances: to comply with applicable laws or regulatory requirements; to fulfil a court order or other legal process; to protect national security or significant public interest.

### 3. How Long We Retain Your Data

We retain your personal data only for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. Once those purposes have been met, we will delete or anonymise your data. If you wish to request earlier deletion, please contact us at any time.

### 4. Your Rights

Under the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA), you have the following rights with respect to your personal data. We are committed to responding within a reasonable timeframe.

**Right to access:** Request access to the personal data we hold about you

**Right to correction:** Request that inaccurate or incomplete data be corrected

**Right to withdrawal of consent:** Withdraw consent you have previously given for us to process your personal data. Withdrawal does not affect processing that took place prior to your withdrawal

**Right to lodge a complaint:** If you believe your personal data rights have been violated, you may lodge a complaint with the Personal Data Protection Commission (PDPC) of Singapore at www.pdpc.gov.sg

To exercise any of the above rights, please contact us at: info@leapin-ai.com

### 5. Minors

This website is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals who are not of legal age to provide consent in their jurisdiction. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

### 6. Updates to This Policy

If we make material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will provide notice on our website and update the effective date at the top of this document. Your continued use of this website after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
